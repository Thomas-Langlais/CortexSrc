Regulatory Reviews Data Collection Template Regulation Information

For organizations with few regulations, complete this template on Regulation Information per individual regulation administered by your organization.

For organizations with numerous regulations, complete this template on Regulation Information per set of regulations. The option to identify a “set of regulations” allows the organization flexibility to deal with a group of regulations in one template where it would be most appropriate. A “regulation set” could be a set of regulations that are managed together (for example, shares a common risk assessment), concern a certain industry (for example, one identified by the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS), a particular regulated activity (for example, licencing), a particular subject matter (for example, pollution) or that have a particular regulatory objective (for example, protection of health). For organizations who choose this option, please identify and list the regulations in the set of regulations in Part 1 of this template and then answer the questions in Part 2 based on the set of regulations. In the event that 10 or more individual regulations need to be listed, please list the regulations in the separate document available in Appendix 1.
